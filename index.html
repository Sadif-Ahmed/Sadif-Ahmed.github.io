Short Biography

Sadif Ahmed is a Lecturer at BRAC University, with a BSc. in Computer Science and Engineering from BUET (2019–2025). His research spans Software Engineering & Security, Large Language Models (LLMs), and Developer Privacy. He has worked on projects related to secret breach detection using hybrid approaches combining regex and LLMs, and contributed to tools and platforms involving blockchain, home automation, and anime data indexing.
